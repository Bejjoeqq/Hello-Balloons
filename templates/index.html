ℹ️ Game Information

🎯 Objective

Navigate your balloon (O) to collect dollars ($) while avoiding spikes (*)!

🎮 Controls

W = Up
A = Left
S = Down
D = Right

⚠️ Warning

When you collect a dollar, a spike will spawn at the previous dollar location!

* * *          * * *
O $     ->     * O
* * *          * * *

💰 Scoring

  • Base points per dollar: 20
  • Bonus points from rage and time
  • Rage bonus lasts 50 moves after collecting
  • Special bonuses every 9th dollar and 4th spike
